# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our standards

Be respectful, direct, and collaborative. Harassment, discrimination, or abusive
behavior is not accepted.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior through a
[GitHub Security Advisory](https://github.com/chayprabs/audio-loudnorm-online/security/advisories/new)
or the maintainer contact on [chaitanyaprabuddha.com](https://www.chaitanyaprabuddha.com).

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and temporarily or permanently
ban participants for behavior they deem inappropriate.
